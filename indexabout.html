About - Card Gallery - Order a Painting

Welcome to Flow Design, here to meet your personalised painting needs. To purchase a personalised painting, submit an order to Holly, describing the request for your painting or card. If you'd like Holly to base her painting on a photograph, please send this to flowdesignbyholly@gmail.com.

For inspiration, browse the Gallery. You can include a design code (shown under the photographs in the gallery) in your order request.

Holly's artwork includes typography, watercolour painting, linework and unique impressions of photographs.

L I V E . I L L U S T R A T I O N

Live illustration is a fun, unique and interactive form of entertainment for weddings and events. Guests receive the cutest hand-painted wedding favours to take home, something special to remember your special event! For more information, contact Holly at flowdesignbyholly@gmail.com.
