Welcome to Flow Design! I'm Holly, a UK Based Artist and Illustrator capturing memories in paint. I love spreading joy by recreating special photographs or illustrating live at weddings and events. I use typography, watercolour painting and linework to create unique and charming artworks. My personalised greetings cards can be sent to you or delivered directly to the recipient with a message printed inside.

- L I V E - I L L U S T R A T I O N -

I illustrate live at weddings and events!

When hired as a Live Illustrator, I attend your event and paint your guests live. Guests recieve their personalised painting on the day, the perfect way to cherish the special moment. It is a unique and interactive form of entertainment that never fails to be popular and spark smiles all round.

For more information or to discuss me attending your event, contact me at flowdesignbyholly@gmail.com or on Instagram @flowdesignbyholly
